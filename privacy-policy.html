Privacy Policy for Mezhaul

Effective Date: August 2025

Mezhaul (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Mezhaul mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password (encrypted).
  • Order & Delivery Information: Delivery address, order details, and contact details for delivery.
  • Location Data: Only when provided by you for delivery purposes. We do not track background location.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system (used only to ensure app functionality).

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Analytics or usage tracking data.
  • Crash reports.
  • Push notification tokens (no push notifications are used).
  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Sensitive personal data such as financial account numbers, government IDs, or health information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To communicate with users about their orders.
  • To support vendor and customer interactions when necessary.
  • To improve customer service.

4. Payments

All payments are processed through PayFast. We do not store or have access to your card or banking information. PayFast may collect necessary information to process payments according to their policies.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We only share necessary information with third parties involved in:

  • Payment processing (PayFast)
  • Order fulfillment
  • Customer communication (email/SMS services if used)

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures such as encrypted passwords and secure HTTPS communication to protect your data. However, no method of transmission is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as required for legal and operational purposes (including order history and tax compliance). You may request deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Update your information.
  • Request deletion of your account.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@mezhaul.co.za
