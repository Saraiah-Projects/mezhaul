Data Subject Rights

At Mezhaul, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Under applicable data protection laws, you have certain rights regarding your personal data.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your data.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a request regarding your data, please contact us at:

Email: info@mezhaul.co.za

Please include your full name, account details (if applicable), and a description of your request. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable laws.
